# New Employee Onboarding Guide (Sample)

This is a fictional sample document for testing the RAG ingestion pipeline. Any names, policies, and figures below are made up.

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Widgets Co.! This guide covers the basics you need for your first two weeks.

## IT Setup

New hires receive a laptop on day one from the IT desk on the 3rd floor. To set it up:

1. Log in with the temporary credentials emailed to your personal address before your start date.
2. Change your password within 24 hours using the Okta portal at go.acmewidgets.internal/okta.
3. Install the VPN client from the Software Center. VPN access is required to reach internal tools from outside the office.
4. Contact the IT helpdesk at it-help@acmewidgets.example or extension 4100 for any setup issues.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue 15 days of PTO per year, credited monthly at 1.25 days. To request time off:

1. Open the HR portal and navigate to "Time Off Requests."
2. Submit requests at least 5 business days in advance for anything longer than 2 days.
3. Your manager must approve the request before it's finalized.
4. Unused PTO up to 5 days can be carried over into the next calendar year; anything beyond that is forfeited on December 31.

## Health Benefits

Health insurance enrollment must be completed within 30 days of your start date through the Benefits portal. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following enrollment. Contact benefits@acmewidgets.example with questions about plan options.

## Who to Contact
